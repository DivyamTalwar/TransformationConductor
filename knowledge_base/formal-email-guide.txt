Formal Email Style Guide:
1. Salutation: Use 'Dear Mr./Ms./Dr. [Last Name]'.
2. Tone: Professional, respectful, and formal.
3. Structure: Begin with a clear statement of purpose. Keep paragraphs focused and concise.
4. Closing: Use 'Sincerely,' or 'Best regards,'.
5. Vocabulary: Avoid slang, contractions, and overly casual phrases.
