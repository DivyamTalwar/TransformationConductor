Blog Post Style Guide:
1. Tone: Conversational, friendly, and engaging.
2. Structure: Use clear headings (H2, H3), short paragraphs, and bullet points.
3. Introduction: Start with a hook to grab the reader's attention.
4. Conclusion: End with a clear takeaway or a question to encourage comments.
5. Vocabulary: Use simple, accessible language. Avoid jargon.
6. Voice: Always use the active voice.
